SILVERGLADES HIGHTOWN 3 & 4 BHK Ultra Luxury Residences

The Silverglades Hightown is a premium residential enclave developed by the makers of ITC Laburnam and Ivy, Gurgaon. Hightown comprises of 3 grand towers in a mixed-use development. The towers offer the best amenities along with unparalleled ease of living. It is spread across 4.85 acres. This iconic residential development offers 260 luxurious residences in 3 & 4 BHK configurations. The apartments are thoughtfully designed to cater to the needs and desires of today’s modern living.

The Silverglades Hightown has an ideal location in a residential neighbourhood in close proximity to The Galleria market and The Golf Course Road. It is well connected to state-of-the-art hospitals, schools, entertainment hubs, shopping and dining destinations. The clubhouse offers amenities and facilities for wellness and recreation. It is a place to rejuvenate and spend quality time with friends and family.
